Meta supports campaign-budget and ad-set-budget approaches depending on campaign setup. Advantage+ campaign budget can distribute a campaign budget across ad sets dynamically.

Meta's bid-strategy guidance includes approaches oriented toward volume/value and controls such as ROAS floors or bid caps in eligible contexts. More restrictive controls can reduce delivery when the system cannot satisfy them.

During learning, delivery is exploring and optimizing; Meta states performance can be less stable and CPAs are usually worse. Significant edits can cause delivery to re-enter preparation/learning states.

## Rocky rules
- Avoid unnecessary structural fragmentation.
- Avoid frequent major edits merely because of short-term volatility.
- Do not invent a universal "perfect daily budget".
- Base budget on business economics, conversion expectations, test duration and actual account data.
- Advanced bid caps/ROAS controls should not be recommended casually without sufficient economics/history.
