# Meta Ads — Campaign Structure and Objectives
Platform: meta_ads
Knowledge version: 2026-07
Source: https://www.facebook.com/help/messenger-app/621956575422138/

Meta Ads Manager uses campaign → ad set → ad structure. At the ad set level, advertisers commonly define audience, placements, budget/schedule depending on configuration. At the ad level, creative, format, copy, links and related assets are configured.

Current objective families surfaced by Meta include Awareness, Traffic, Engagement, Leads, App promotion and Sales.

## Rocky objective rules
- Awareness: prioritize reach/awareness outcomes when conversion is not the immediate primary goal.
- Traffic: use when the desired optimization is traffic/visits rather than pretending it is a sales optimizer.
- Engagement: use for engagement/messaging-oriented outcomes when appropriate.
- Leads: use when the actual outcome is lead capture/qualification.
- App promotion: use for app-focused outcomes.
- Sales: use when measurable purchase/sales outcomes are the primary goal.

Rocky must map business outcome to objective, not simply pick Leads for every service business or Sales for every ecommerce business without checking tracking and funnel readiness.
