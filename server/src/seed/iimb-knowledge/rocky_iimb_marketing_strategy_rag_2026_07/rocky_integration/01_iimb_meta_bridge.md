# Rocky Bridge — IIMB Strategy + Meta Ads Mechanics
Knowledge version: 2026-07

This collection does NOT claim to be an official "IIM Bangalore Meta Ads course database."

Use two knowledge layers:
1. IIMB-derived strategy knowledge: customer behaviour, segmentation, positioning, marketing analytics, ROI, communication and research logic.
2. Rocky's official Meta Ads RAG: current objectives, audience mechanics, placements, bidding, Pixel/CAPI, policies and platform constraints.

## Retrieval sequence for a Meta campaign
A. Retrieve customer/segment/positioning knowledge from this IIMB-derived collection.
B. Analyze business economics and funnel.
C. Form campaign hypotheses.
D. Retrieve current Meta platform mechanics from official Meta collection.
E. Translate strategy into objective, optimization, audience controls/signals, placements, creative requirements and measurement.
F. Validate policy/privacy.
G. Produce plan with explicit assumptions and missing data.

This prevents old academic/platform examples from overriding current Meta mechanics.
