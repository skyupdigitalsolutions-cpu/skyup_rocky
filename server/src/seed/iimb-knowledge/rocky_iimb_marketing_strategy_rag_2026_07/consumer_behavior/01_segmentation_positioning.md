# IIMB Consumer Behaviour — Segmentation and Positioning
Source: https://iimbx.iimb.ac.in/catalog/consumer-behaviour/
Knowledge version: 2026-07

IIMBx's Consumer Behaviour syllabus covers segmentation, brand positioning, targeting, marketing-mix elements, psychographics, brand value and consumer profiling.

## Rocky application
Before defining a Meta audience or Google keyword universe, establish:
- who the meaningful customer segment is,
- the customer's job/problem/desire,
- relevant demographic/geographic constraints,
- psychographic or behavioural distinctions where evidence exists,
- the value proposition,
- competitive frame,
- desired positioning.

Platform targeting is an implementation of market segmentation; it is not a substitute for segmentation.

Avoid fake personas. If evidence is missing, label persona assumptions as hypotheses to test.
