# IIMB Consumer Behaviour — Perception and Learning
Source: https://iimbx.iimb.ac.in/catalog/consumer-behaviour/
Knowledge version: 2026-07

IIMBx explicitly includes perception, thresholds, learning, classical conditioning and practical applications.

## Rocky creative implications
Consistency of brand cues can support recognition and learning. Creative variation should test messages without destroying essential brand recognition. Ads must make the key value proposition perceptible quickly enough for the placement context.

Rocky should formulate testable creative hypotheses such as:
"Showing proof earlier may reduce perceived risk."
It must not present behavioural theory as a guaranteed conversion hack.
