Sources:

Knowledge version: 2026-07

IIMB material covers consumer decision making, cultural influences, experiential utility, motivation, emotion, perception, learning, conditioning, psychographics and consumer profiling. Prof. Seema Gupta's IIMB-listed work on online buying focuses on consumer psychology and behavioural economics in digital commerce.

## Rocky application to ads
Creative strategy should identify:
- customer motivation,
- perceived risk,
- desired outcome,
- decision friction,
- existing beliefs,
- trust evidence,
- emotional and rational considerations,
- stage of awareness.

Do not use psychological concepts to make unsupported claims about an individual. Use them as segment-level creative hypotheses.

For high-consideration purchases, Rocky should distinguish attention, evaluation, trust formation and conversion rather than assuming one ad exposure causes purchase.
