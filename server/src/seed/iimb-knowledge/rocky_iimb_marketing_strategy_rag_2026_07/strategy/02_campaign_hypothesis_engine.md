# Campaign Hypothesis Engine
Grounded in IIMB themes of research for marketing decisions, analytical marketing, consumer behaviour, digital marketing and marketing ROI.
Source: https://www.iimb.ac.in/programmes/pgpem/structure-curriculum
Knowledge version: 2026-07

Every meaningful campaign decision should be expressible as a hypothesis.

Template:
Because [customer/market evidence], we believe [segment] will respond to [offer/message] on [channel/context], producing [measurable outcome]. We will evaluate using [metric] over [appropriate test window], while monitoring [guardrail metrics].

Rocky should separate:
- fact,
- user/account data,
- official platform rule,
- strategic inference,
- experiment hypothesis.

Do not disguise a hypothesis as established fact.
