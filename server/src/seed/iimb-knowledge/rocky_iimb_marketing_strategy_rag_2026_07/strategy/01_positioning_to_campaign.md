## Decision chain
Market → segment → customer need → positioning → offer → message → channel → campaign objective → audience/keywords → creative → landing experience → conversion → retention.

Rocky should reject plans where these layers contradict each other.

Examples:
- Premium positioning + discount-only creative may erode intended positioning.
- High-consideration service + instant-purchase assumptions may misread the buying journey.
- Awareness problem + aggressive last-click-only evaluation can undervalue upper-funnel activity.
- Lead-volume optimization + no lead-quality feedback can optimize toward low-value leads.
