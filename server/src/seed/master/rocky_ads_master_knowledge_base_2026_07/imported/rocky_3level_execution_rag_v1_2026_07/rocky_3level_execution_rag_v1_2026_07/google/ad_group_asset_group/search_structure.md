# Google Search Ad Group Structure
Platform: google_ads
Level: ad_group
Topic: search_structure
Source: https://developers.google.com/google-ads/api/fields/v22/ad_group
Verified: 2026-07-24

Google Ads ad groups expose bidding, settings, status, relationships and performance data. Rocky should group search intent coherently so ads and landing pages match the underlying intent.

Avoid excessive micro-groups without a relevance, reporting or testing reason.
