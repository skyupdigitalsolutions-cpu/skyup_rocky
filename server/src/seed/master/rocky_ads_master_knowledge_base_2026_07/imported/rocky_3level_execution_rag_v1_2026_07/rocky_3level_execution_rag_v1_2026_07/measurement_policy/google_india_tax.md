# Google Ads India Tax Operational Note
Platform: google_ads
Level: account
Topic: tax
Country: IN
Source: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/2375370?hl=en
Verified: 2026-07-24

Google's current India documentation lists CGST 9% + SGST 9% for applicable intra-state treatment and IGST 18% for applicable inter-state treatment, with documented exceptions including eligible SEZ treatment.

This is an operational note, not tax advice.
