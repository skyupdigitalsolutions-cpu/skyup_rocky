# Google Campaign Type Selection
Platform: google_ads
Level: campaign
Topic: campaign_type
Source: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/2567043?hl=en
Verified: 2026-07-24

Google campaign selection starts from goals and campaign type. Search captures active search demand. Performance Max is goal-based and can access inventory across Google channels using Smart Bidding. Other types serve different inventory and objectives.

Rocky decision order: business outcome → conversion goal → demand state → inventory/creative fit → campaign type. Do not choose PMax merely because it spans channels, or Search when the core job is primarily visual demand creation.
