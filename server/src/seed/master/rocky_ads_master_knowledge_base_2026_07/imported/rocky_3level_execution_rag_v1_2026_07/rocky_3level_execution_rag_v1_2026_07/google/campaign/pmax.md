# Google Performance Max
Platform: google_ads
Level: campaign
Topic: performance_max
Sources:

Verified: 2026-07-24

Performance Max is goal-based. Audience signals are suggestions that guide Google AI, not exclusive hard targeting. Search themes provide words and phrases describing likely customer searches and additional business context; they are not traditional Search keywords.

Rocky must never represent audience signals or search themes as deterministic targeting.
