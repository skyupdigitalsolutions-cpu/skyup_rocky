# Performance Max Search Themes
Platform: google_ads
Level: asset_group
Topic: search_themes
Source: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/16669486?hl=en
Verified: 2026-07-24

Search themes communicate business and customer search context to Performance Max. They differ from traditional keywords and act as AI guidance.

Use them for useful context that landing pages, feeds or assets may not communicate clearly. Do not treat them as exact Search targeting.
