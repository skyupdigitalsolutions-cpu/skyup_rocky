# Performance Max Asset Specifications
Platform: google_ads
Level: ad
Topic: asset_specs
Source: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/17091269?hl=en
Verified: 2026-07-24

Google's current official PMax specification states headline assets allow up to 30 characters, with at least 3 and at most 15 headlines for the relevant non-feed asset-group requirement. Other asset types have separate requirements.

Rocky must retrieve current specifications before producing a final executable creative manifest. Never generalize one asset type's limits to another format.
