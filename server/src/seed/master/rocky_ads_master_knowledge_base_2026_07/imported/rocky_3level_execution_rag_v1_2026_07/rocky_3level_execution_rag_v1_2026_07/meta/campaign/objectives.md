# Meta Campaign Objectives
Platform: meta_ads
Level: campaign
Topic: objective_selection
Source: https://www.facebook.com/business/ads/ad-objectives
Verified: 2026-07-24

Meta's auction uses the selected objective to pursue the result the advertiser wants. Rocky must map the real business outcome to the closest platform objective rather than choosing from industry alone.

Rules:
- Do not use Traffic when the real primary outcome is measurable leads or sales merely because a website is involved.
- Do not default every service business to Leads without resolving the actual conversion journey.
- Keep the business objective separate from the Meta objective.
- Validate objective, conversion location and performance-goal compatibility against the current platform before API creation.
