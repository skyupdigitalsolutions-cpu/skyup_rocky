# Google Performance Max Asset Groups
Platform: google_ads
Level: asset_group
Topic: pmax_assets
Sources:
- https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/17091269?hl=en
- https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/14530785
Verified: 2026-07-24

Asset groups combine creative inputs and can include audience signals. Signals guide machine learning but do not constrain delivery exclusively to those users.

Build asset groups around coherent business/creative themes. Avoid nearly identical duplication without a measurement reason.
