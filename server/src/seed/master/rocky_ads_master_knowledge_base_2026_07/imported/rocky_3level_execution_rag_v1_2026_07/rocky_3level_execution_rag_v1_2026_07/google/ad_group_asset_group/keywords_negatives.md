# Google Keywords and Negative Keywords
Platform: google_ads
Level: ad_group
Topic: keywords
Source: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/2453972
Verified: 2026-07-24

Negative keywords exclude unwanted search intent. Google documents broad, phrase and exact negative matching and notes that negative matching differs from positive matching.

Rocky must derive keyword themes from the real offer and search intent, separate materially different intent, and exclude clearly irrelevant meanings. Never invent search volume, CPC, competition or forecasts; use live Keyword Planner/account data for numerical evidence.
