# Google Bidding and Budget Guardrails
Platform: google_ads
Level: campaign
Topic: bidding_budget

Select bidding according to the optimization goal and quality of conversion/value data. Never invent Target CPA or Target ROAS from generic benchmarks.

When reliable historical economics are unavailable, an initial approach may be proposed only as STRATEGY_INFERENCE. Connected account history and live forecasts take precedence over generic assumptions.
