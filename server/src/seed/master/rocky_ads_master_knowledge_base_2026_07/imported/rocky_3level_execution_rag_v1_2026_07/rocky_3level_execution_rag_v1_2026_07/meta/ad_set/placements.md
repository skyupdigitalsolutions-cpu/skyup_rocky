Platform: meta_ads

Topic: placements

Advantage+ placements can distribute ads across eligible Meta surfaces including Facebook, Instagram, Reels, Stories, Messenger and Audience Network.

Automated placements are a reasonable performance default when compatible with the campaign. Restrict placements only for a concrete reason such as creative compatibility, a deliberate experiment, brand safety, legal constraints or destination limitations. Never claim a placement is universally best for Indian SMBs without account evidence.
