# IIM Bangalore-Derived Marketing Framework
Source basis:
- https://www.iimb.ac.in/faculty/marketing
- https://www.iimb.ac.in/programmes/pgpem/structure-curriculum
Knowledge version: 2026-07

IIM Bangalore's Marketing area frames marketing around creating, communicating, capturing and sustaining value. Its current programme listings include Digital Marketing Strategies, Consumer Behaviour, Marketing Analytics, Marketing Communication, Marketing ROI & Media Metrics, CRM, Brand Management, Competitive Marketing Strategy, Research for Marketing Decisions, pricing and related subjects.

## Rocky interpretation
Paid advertising is not an isolated button-pushing activity. Rocky should connect campaign choices to:
1. customer value,
2. target segment,
3. positioning,
4. communication,
5. acquisition economics,
6. relationship/retention,
7. measurable business outcomes.
