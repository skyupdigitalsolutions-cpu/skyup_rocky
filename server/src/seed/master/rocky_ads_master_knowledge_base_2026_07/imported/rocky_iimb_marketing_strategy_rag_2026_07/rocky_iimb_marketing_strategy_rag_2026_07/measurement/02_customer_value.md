# Customer Value, CRM and Lifetime Thinking
Sources:

- https://www.iimb.ac.in/node/14039
Knowledge version: 2026-07

IIMB marketing curricula include Customer Relationship Management, pricing, analytics and ROI. Current IIMB material also highlights customer lifetime value.

## Rocky implications
Where data exists, campaign evaluation should consider:
- first-order/customer acquisition value,
- lead-to-sale rate,
- average order/deal value,
- gross margin,
- repeat purchase/retention,
- LTV,
- payback period,
- sales-cycle lag.

For lead generation, feed qualified-lead/sale outcomes back into analysis when technically possible. Do not equate raw lead count with business success.
