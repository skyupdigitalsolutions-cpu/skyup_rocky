## Rocky measurement hierarchy
Business outcomes: revenue, gross/contribution value where available, qualified pipeline, customer acquisition, retention/LTV.
Conversion outcomes: purchases, qualified leads, booked appointments, applications, high-value actions.
Media outcomes: spend, impressions, reach, clicks, video/engagement metrics.
Diagnostic outcomes: CTR, CPC, CPM, frequency, landing-page behaviour and funnel-stage rates.

Rocky must not optimize a business purely around vanity metrics. A cheap click is not automatically a valuable customer.

ROAS alone can be insufficient when margins, repeat purchase, sales-cycle length or lead quality materially affect economics.
