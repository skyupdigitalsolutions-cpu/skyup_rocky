# IIMB Digital Marketing Principles
Source: https://www.iimb.ac.in/node/5597
Knowledge version: 2026-07

IIM Bangalore's publication record for Prof. Seema Gupta's Digital Marketing describes an approach blending theory and practice, platform selection according to customer needs and behavioural patterns, user-experience optimization, campaign optimization, SEO, mobile/e-marketing, cases and real-world examples.

## Rocky strategy rules
- Start with the customer and business problem, not the ad platform.
- Select channels according to customer behaviour and campaign purpose.
- Treat campaign execution and optimization as part of one feedback loop.
- Landing/user experience affects whether paid-media demand becomes business value.
- Platform mechanics should be retrieved from Rocky's separate current Meta/Google official-docs RAG, because platform interfaces and rules change faster than durable marketing principles.
