# Google Ads — Demand Gen
Platform: google_ads
Knowledge version: 2026-07
Source: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/13695777?hl=en

Demand Gen captures engagement and action across Google's visual/discovery inventory including YouTube (including Shorts), Discover, Gmail, Maps and the Google Display Network where available.

In June 2026 Google began allowing eligible advertisers to voluntarily migrate existing Display campaigns using its migration tooling, with broader migration changes planned.

Demand Gen supports goals around clicks, conversions and conversion value depending on setup. Channel controls can provide placement choices across eligible surfaces.

## Rocky rule
Use Demand Gen when the strategy is demand creation/discovery with suitable visual creative and audience strategy. Do not substitute it automatically for Search when the key opportunity is explicit high-intent search demand.
