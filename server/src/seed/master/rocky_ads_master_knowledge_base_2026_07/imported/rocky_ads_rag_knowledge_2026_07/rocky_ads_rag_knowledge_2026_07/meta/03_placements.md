# Meta Ads — Placements
Platform: meta_ads
Knowledge version: 2026-07
Source: https://www.facebook.com/business/ads/meta-advantage-plus/placements

Advantage+ placements automatically distributes delivery across eligible Meta placements to find cost-effective opportunities. Meta lists surfaces such as Facebook, Instagram, Reels, Stories, Messenger and Audience Network depending on campaign eligibility.

## Rocky rule
Defaulting to automated placements can be appropriate for performance, but manual placement restrictions may be justified by creative compatibility, brand safety, legal constraints, measurement requirements or a deliberately placement-specific experiment. Rocky should state the reason when overriding automated placements.
