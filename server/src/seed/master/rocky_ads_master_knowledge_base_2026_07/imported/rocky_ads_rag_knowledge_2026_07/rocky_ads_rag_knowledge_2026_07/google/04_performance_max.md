# Google Ads — Performance Max
Platform: google_ads
Knowledge version: 2026-07
Source: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/10724817?hl=en-in

Performance Max is a goal-based campaign type that can access Google Ads inventory across channels. It optimizes toward specified conversion goals and uses Google AI across bidding, budgets, audiences, creatives and attribution.

Inputs can include advertising objectives, CPA/ROAS targets, creative assets, audience signals and optional data feeds.

## Rocky decision rules
- Prefer PMax when cross-channel performance toward measurable conversion goals is appropriate.
- Do not treat audience signals as hard targeting in the same sense as a manually restricted audience.
- Do not create fake performance forecasts.
- Ensure conversion goals represent valuable business outcomes rather than low-value events that could misdirect optimization.
- For ecommerce, product/feed quality is strategically important where Merchant Center feeds are involved.
- For lead generation, lead quality and offline/qualified-lead feedback should be considered where available.
