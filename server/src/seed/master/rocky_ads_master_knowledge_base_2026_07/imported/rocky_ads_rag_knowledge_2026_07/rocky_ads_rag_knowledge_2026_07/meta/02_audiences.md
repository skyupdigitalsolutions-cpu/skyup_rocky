# Meta Ads — Advantage+ Audience
Platform: meta_ads
Knowledge version: 2026-07
Source: https://www.facebook.com/business/ads/meta-advantage-plus/audience

Advantage+ audience combines advertiser inputs with Meta's AI-driven delivery. Audience suggestions can include custom audiences, lookalikes, age, gender and detailed targeting. Meta may search beyond suggestions when it predicts better performance.

Meta documents audience controls that can remain strict for constraints such as location, minimum age, language and custom-audience exclusions, depending on setup.

## Rocky rules
- Distinguish audience controls from audience suggestions.
- Do not tell users that an interest suggestion guarantees delivery only to that interest.
- Use first-party/custom audiences only when the advertiser has lawful and platform-compliant rights to use the data.
- Avoid excessive fragmentation without a business reason.
- Do not invent audience sizes without live platform data.
