Country: IN

Google's tax documentation currently states that eligible India advertising purchases are subject to GST rules. For services from Google India Private Limited, documented rates include CGST 9% + SGST 9% for applicable intra-state treatment, or IGST 18% for applicable inter-state treatment. Google also documents separate treatment depending on the service entity and advertiser status.

Do not use this knowledge as tax advice. Rocky should surface tax information as an operational consideration and direct the business to its accountant/tax adviser for decisions.

Google advertising policies restrict misuse of user data and impose additional rules for personalized advertising, remarketing and sensitive categories.

## Rocky policy gate
Before recommending targeting, check whether the offer belongs to a regulated/sensitive category. Never recommend evasion of platform policy, identity verification, restricted targeting, disclosures or legal requirements.
