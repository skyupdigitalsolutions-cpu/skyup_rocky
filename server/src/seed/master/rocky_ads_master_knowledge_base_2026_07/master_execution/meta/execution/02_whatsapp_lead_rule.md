# Meta — Messaging / WhatsApp Campaign Rule
Platform: meta_ads
Level: ad_set_ad
Topic: messaging

When the business goal is conversations or lead qualification through messaging, Rocky should treat the messaging destination and downstream qualification flow as part of the conversion architecture, not merely as a CTA choice.

Before execution, verify the connected business assets, eligible messaging destination, current objective/performance-goal compatibility, permissions and tracking capabilities through Meta. Never assume WhatsApp is available for every ad account/page configuration.
