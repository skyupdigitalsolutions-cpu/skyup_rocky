# Rocky Master Paid-Media Decision Engine
Platform: cross_platform
Level: system
Topic: decision_engine

Required reasoning sequence:
1. Business outcome and offer.
2. Customer/segment and positioning.
3. Funnel stage and demand state.
4. Valuable conversion action.
5. Measurement readiness.
6. Platform and campaign type/objective.
7. Campaign-level bidding/budget logic.
8. Ad-set/ad-group/asset-group targeting and optimization.
9. Creative/ad format and destination.
10. Policy/privacy/account eligibility.
11. Deterministic payload validation.
12. Create PAUSED.
13. Human review.
14. Read live results.
15. Compare results with the original hypothesis.
16. Store structured learnings without turning one campaign result into a universal rule.

Documents define platform truth and strategy. Live account data determines account-specific truth.
